# AK Digital Services — Outreach Scripts

---

## 📞 COLD CALL SCRIPT

**Use when:** Calling businesses directly from the lead finder list.

---

**[When they answer]**

> "Hey, is this the owner of [Business Name]?"

**[Yes]**

> "Hey [Name], my name is AK. I run a digital marketing agency here in Austin. I was actually looking for a [plumber / electrician / handyman] in the area and I came across your business on Google — I noticed you guys don't have a website yet. I help local service businesses like yours get found online and get more calls. Is that something you'd be open to hearing more about? Takes two minutes."

---

**[If they say "not interested" or "too busy"]**

> "No worries at all — I'll tell you what, let me send you a quick text with some info and if it ever makes sense down the road, you'll have it. What's the best number to reach you?"

---

**[If they say "tell me more"]**

> "So basically what I do is build you a professional website, get you showing up on Google when people search for [their service] in Austin, and set up a system that automatically follows up with anyone who calls and doesn't get an answer. Most of my clients start getting more calls within the first 30 days. I work with a lot of local service businesses — plumbers, electricians, handymen — and the results are pretty consistent. Can I send you some examples of sites I've built and a quick breakdown of pricing?"

---

**[Close]**

> "Awesome. What's your email? I'll shoot that over today. And honestly the best way to see if it's a good fit is just a quick 15-minute call — are you free sometime this week?"

## 💬 COLD TEXT SCRIPT

**Use when:** Texting businesses (higher open rate than calls for some owners)

---

**First text:**

> "Hey [Name], my name is AK. I help local service businesses in Austin get more customers through websites and Google. I noticed [Business Name] doesn't have a website yet — I've helped businesses like yours go from 0 to getting consistent calls online. Mind if I send you some examples?"

---

**If they reply yes / tell me more:**

> "Awesome! Here's a quick look at what I do: I build professional websites starting at $500, set you up on Google so customers can find you, and add an AI system that texts back anyone who calls and doesn't get an answer. Most clients see results within 30 days. Would you be open to a quick 15-min call this week to see if it's a fit?"

---

**If no reply after 2 days — follow up:**

> "Hey [Name], just following up on my last message. No pressure at all — just wanted to make sure you got it. Happy to answer any questions if you have them."

## 🛑 OBJECTION HANDLERS

**"I already get enough business from referrals"**
> "That's great — referrals are the best. My clients usually say the same thing, but what we find is a website makes those referrals close faster. When someone gets referred to you and Googles your name, if nothing comes up, some of them move on. A website just backs up the reputation you already have."

**"I can't afford it right now"**
> "I totally get it. That's actually why I structured my pricing the way I did — the starter site is $500 one time, and I offer payment plans. But honestly, if one extra job comes in because of the site, it's already paid for itself. What's a typical job worth for you?"

**"I'll think about it"**
> "Of course, no rush. Can I ask — what would need to be true for it to be a yes? Just want to make sure I'm giving you the right info to make the decision."

**"I had a bad experience with marketing before"**
> "I hear that a lot honestly. A lot of agencies overpromise and underdeliver. That's why I offer a first month discount — I want you to see results before you're fully committed. What happened last time, if you don't mind me asking?"

|-----|--------|

**Day 14 closing the loop:**
> "Hey [Name], AK here — last time I'll reach out, promise. Just wanted to close the loop on my earlier message. If the timing ever works out, I'd love to help [Business Name] get more customers online. Feel free to reach out anytime. Take care!"
