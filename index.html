Tip: Open the lead finder, pick a business with no website, then use this script. Keep it conversational — you're a local guy helping local businesses.

Step 1 — Opener

Say this

"Hey, is this the owner of [Business Name]?"

Step 2 — The Pitch

When they say yes

"Hey [Name], my name is AK. I run a digital marketing agency here in Austin. I was looking for a [plumber / electrician / handyman] in the area and I found your business on Google — I noticed you don't have a website yet. I help local service businesses get found online and get more calls. Is that something you'd be open to hearing more about? Takes two minutes."

Step 3 — If They Want More Info

When they say tell me more

"So I build you a professional website, get you showing up on Google when people search for [their service] in Austin, and set up a system that automatically texts back anyone who calls and doesn't get an answer — so you never lose a lead. Most of my clients start getting more calls within the first 30 days. Can I send you some examples and pricing?"

Step 4 — Close

Get the meeting

"Awesome. What's your email? I'll shoot that over today. And the best way to see if it's a fit is a quick 15-minute call — are you free sometime this week?"

If They're Not Interested

Soft exit

"No worries at all — let me send you a quick text with some info and if it ever makes sense down the road, you'll have it. What's the best number?"

Tip: Texts have higher open rates than calls. Send this first, then call if they don't reply in 24 hours.

First Text

Copy & send

"Hey [Name], my name is AK. I help local service businesses in Austin get more customers through websites and Google. I noticed [Business Name] doesn't have a website — I've helped businesses like yours go from 0 to getting consistent calls online. Mind if I send you some examples?"

If They Reply Yes

Follow up

"Awesome! I build professional websites starting at $500, set you up on Google so customers find you, and add an AI system that texts back anyone who calls and doesn't get an answer. Most clients see results in 30 days. Would you be open to a quick 15-min call this week?"

No Reply After 2 Days

Follow up text

"Hey [Name], just following up on my last message. No pressure — just wanted to make sure you got it. Happy to answer any questions!"

Subject Line

More customers for [Business Name] — quick question

Email Body

Hi [Name],

My name is AK and I run a digital marketing agency in Austin focused on local service businesses.

I came across [Business Name] and noticed you don't have a website yet. I specialize in helping businesses like yours get found on Google and turn more calls into paying customers.

Here's what I do:
• Build a clean, fast website that shows up on Google
• Set up your Google Business Profile
• Add an AI system that auto-texts back missed calls

Would you be open to a quick 15-minute call? No pitch, no pressure.

— AK
AK Digital Services | Austin, TX

Tip: Don't argue. Agree, then redirect. These responses keep the conversation alive.

Common Objections

❝ I already get enough business from referrals ❞
"That's great — referrals are the best. But what we find is a website makes those referrals close faster. When someone gets referred to you and Googles your name, if nothing comes up, some of them move on. A website just backs up the reputation you already have."
❝ I can't afford it right now ❞
"I totally get it. The starter site is $500 one time and I offer payment plans. But honestly — what's a typical job worth for you? If one extra customer comes in because of the site, it's already paid for itself."
❝ I'll think about it ❞
"Of course, no rush. Can I ask — what would need to be true for it to be a yes? Just want to make sure I'm giving you the right info."
❝ I had a bad experience with marketing before ❞
"I hear that a lot. A lot of agencies overpromise. That's why I offer a first month discount — I want you to see results before you're fully committed. What happened last time?"
Tip: Most sales happen on the 3rd-5th contact. Don't give up after one try.
