Panel Upgrades

Your electrical panel is the heart of your home's power system. An outdated or overloaded panel is a fire hazard. We upgrade to modern 200A panels safely, to code, and with permits pulled — so your home is protected and your insurance stays valid.

  • 100A to 200A upgrades
  • Sub-panel installation
  • Breaker replacement
  • Code compliance updates

Outlet & Wiring

Whether you need a single new outlet or a full rewire on an older home, we handle it clean. Every outlet is grounded, tamper-resistant where required, and installed to last. We also run dedicated circuits for high-draw appliances like dryers, ranges, and home offices.

  • GFCI and AFCI outlets
  • Dedicated circuits
  • Whole-home rewiring
  • USB outlet upgrades

Lighting Installation

  • Smart switch installation

EV Charger Installation

Stop relying on a slow Level 1 trickle charger. We install Level 2 EV chargers that fully charge most vehicles overnight. We handle the city permit, panel assessment, conduit run, and final hookup — one call, done right.

  • All major brands supported
  • Permit pulled and inspected
  • Panel upgrade if needed
